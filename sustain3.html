Architecture Subsysytem Event

  1. Home
  2. SubSystem
  3. Architecture

The increasing availability and usability of tools for daylight and energy simulation into parametric and generative design software allow the integration of environmental factors into the design process from the very early stages. Such tools must be understood and appreciated for improving the efficiency of the design processes and the performance of the buildings. These tools give the designers new methodologies of work to create environmentally-aware and high performance architectural and spatial design.


About the Speaker

  • Supriya Gadre is an architect by profession. She has recently graduated in Parametric Design in Architecture at UPC in Barcelona, Spain. She was a research assistant in Dept. of Energy science & engineering in IIT Bombay and part of Team SHUNYA in 2017.
