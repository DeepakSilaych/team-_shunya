    Civil Subsystem includes the students interested in or studying construction management, construction materials, and structural analysis of the building. The civil division is responsible for the structural design and creating drawings in collaboration with architects. Furthermore, in collaboration with the mechanical subsystem, the team selects materials after multiple energy simulations. Preparing BIM and implementing the Construction Management Plan is mainly handled by the Civil Subsystem.
