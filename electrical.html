Electrical Requirements

Electrical Subsystem is responsible for electrical requirements of the house such as energy demands, wiring and home appliances. Aim of Electrical Subsystem is to achieve net positive energy status for the house while providing state of the art facilities.

Tasks handled

  • Photovoltaics system and associated inverter design
  • Home Automation
  • Selection of Home Appliances
  • Design of Home Appliances (not readily available in market)
  • Electrical Wiring of the house

Skills required from subsystem members

  • C/C++
  • Embedded coding
  • Eagle CAD
  • PVSYST
  • MATLAB
