Our Work

To check the Financial and Market viability of a project by Target Market Analysis, Cost analysis of construction, Comparative Life Cycle cost Analysis and Financial Feasibility Analysis.

Objectives

  • Execution of market analysis, including affordability and the integration of critical findings in the design
  • Use of design solutions that meet current market expectations for owner experience
  • Application of market-ready construction materials and their cost-effectiveness in the design
  • Life cycle cost comparison between a minimally code-compliant building to the proposed design
  • Financial feasibility analysis in the target market as presented to the consumer
  • Operational and maintenance cost estimate

Skills and Softwares

  • Knowledge of each stage in Construction which includes integration of Civil, Electrical and Mechanical sub systerms
  • Primavera P6
  • CANDY - Construction Estimating Software
  • Quantity Surveying
  • Market survey techniques
  • Construction Finance and Economics
  • Valuation of Land, Property and Building
  • Contract Management
