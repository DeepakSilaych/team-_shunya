Previous Participations and Marketing history of the Team

  • Previously we represented India in two Solar Decathlon competitions.
  • First was Solar Decathlon Europe 2014 which was hosted by France. The house we built for the competition “Project H0” is an 800 sq.feet single storied modular house with modern amenities.
  • Jet Airways was the title sponsor of the Project H0. Moreover, Saint-Gobain, Ingersoll Rand, Larsen & Toubro, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation of India(ONGC) were leading sponsors of the Team.
  • We received the “Honorable Mention” award in sustainability in the competition. During the course of the project, our Team registered two patents and published eight research papers.
  • In 2018, we were part of Solar Decathlon China with our project Solarise. Solarise is an 1800 sq. Feet G+1 net positive energy smart house.
  • Reliance Industries and Lloyd Insulations(India) were title sponsors, and Waaree Energy Systems, Armstrong World Industries(India), Pepperfry, L&T Electrical & Automation, BSH Household Appliances Manufacturing, Covestro(India) were leading sponsors of the Team.
  • We received the “Best Participant Award” for our work.
