Materials Subsysytem Event

  1. Home
  2. SubSystem
  3. Materials

Materials development for various functionalities till date has been taking place without due consideration to various factors such as; environmental impact, resource availability and sustainability. This mode of development has led to a complete dependence on several materials which have become crucial for technological sustenance. Hence, the materials development strategy requires a course correction so that they are on a truly ‘sustainable path’. The present talk will bring out various facts that show clearly the dependence on materials and their impact on environment with specific cases. A path for sustainable development was dicussed.


About the Speaker

  • Professor Satish Vitta is a professor in Metallurgical engineering and Material Science department in IIT Bombay for around 30 years now. He has more than hundred publications mainly on material science.
